About the Series

Math Has Never
Looked Like This

Axel and Ada don't teach math. They live it. Every episode drops them in a new city somewhere in the world — and every city hides a mathematical concept in its streets, its people, its food, and its chaos.

The math is always there. Ada always finds it. Axel always acts surprised.


Part travel comic. Part math adventure. Completely addictive. No textbooks. No classrooms. No boring explanations.

"If you ever thought math wasn't for you — Axel thought so too."

The Characters

Meet the Prime Pair

Two minds. Infinite solutions. One sees the world in patterns. The other sees it in shapes. Together they cover all of mathematics.

A
Female Lead

ADA

The Analytical Genius

Sharp, unbothered, and always three steps ahead. Ada approaches every problem analytically — she sees patterns in markets, rhythms in samba, and equations in café bills. She doesn't teach math. She reveals it.

"The answer was always in the data."
Algebraic Pattern-driven Unbothered Analytical Always right
X
Male Lead

AXEL

The Bold Problem-Solver

Street-smart, confident, and convinced he already knows everything he needs to know. Until Ada shows him he doesn't. Axel approaches the world geometrically — visually, spatially, instinctively. He's faster than he thinks.

"Every problem has an axis — you just have to find it."
Geometric Spatial Instinctive Bold Underestimates himself
